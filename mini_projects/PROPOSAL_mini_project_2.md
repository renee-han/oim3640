## My Project Proposal

## What I'm building: [For my second mini project, I want to build an analyzer tool that is centered in the CPG (consumer packaged goods) industry where it focuses on promotional text and product descriptions of brands from healthy vs. 'indulgent' companies to see the difference in the kinds of terms and language that both of these types of products use. The goal of this project is to provide not only end users, but potentially, CPG companies an opportunity to see how different types of brands position themselves and use different strategies to market their products to various types of consumers.]

## Why I'm building it: [The reason why I'm building this is because the CPG industry is one where I hope to launch my future career and by doing this project, I believe it will set me up in a better position to understand the different companies that are out there and the brand insight nuances that are utilized to capture and attain customer interest/retention. By translating raw text into meaningful insight, it prepares me as a future business analyst to be able to provide actionable recommendations across product categories and diverse audiences.]

## Notable features:[1. Product descriptions from launch campaigns or company websites from 2-3 healthy and indulgent companies 2. Word frequency 3. Top words ]
